How to make Chicken Curry

Ingredients:

  1. For marinating
    • a few leaves of mint(optional)
    • 2lb of chicken breast
    • 1 tbsp Ginger Garlic Paste
    • 2 tsp of chili powder
    • 1 lime/lemon juice
    • 1 cup of curd
    • 2 tsp of Salt
    • 2 tbsp of oil
    • 2 tsp of tumeric
  2. For making gravy
    • 1 Onion
    • 3 tomatoes
    • 2 tsp of chili powder
    • 1 tbsp Ginger Garlic Paste
    • 2 tbsp of oil
    • 2 tsp of Garam Masala
    • 2 tsp of Chicken curry Masala(optional)
    • 3 chili peppers(optional)
    • 3 cloves(optional)
    • 3 cardamom(optional)
    • 1 cinnamon stick(optional)
    • 2 tsp of tumeric(optional)
    • 2 bay leaves(optional)

Preparation:

Marinating Chicken(phase1)

  1. Cut chicken into fair small 2in by 2in slices
  2. In a bowl, add the following...
    • tumeric
    • salt
    • chili pepper
    • lime/lemmon juice
    • curd
    • oil
  3. Mix everything well and then leave in the fridge overnight

Preparing the Curry(phase2)

  1. Cut onions and puree the tomatoes into small pieces
  2. Add the oil into a medium sized pan
  3. Once the oil is hot add the bay leaf, cloves, cinammon, and cardamom
  4. When you can start smelling the spices, add the ginger-garlic paste
  5. After 20 seconds, add onions and then a pinch of salt to allow them to caramelize
  6. Add the Garam Masala and Chicken Curry Masala. Let the curry cook for 5 minutes
  7. Before adding the chicken, taste the broth and add additional salt and chili powder based on your spice tolerence
  8. Add the chicken into the pot, mix, and then put a lid on
  9. Check up on the curry in 5 minute intervals until the chicken is half cooked
  10. Add the tomatoe puree. You will know everything is cooked when oil is oozing out
  11. After everything is cooked and tastes good, you may add cilantro as a garnish
  12. This curry can be eaten with rice or roti.
